Welcome to Sangeetha's webpage :)

I am a third-year graduate student at Univeristy of Wisconsin-Madison. My interests lie in the intersection of Physics-based simulation and High-performance computing. I am particularly interested in designing high-scale physics-simulations. I also find it cool to augment physics with some deep learning techniques to teach computers physics! I am fortunate to work with my advisor, Prof. Eftychios Sifakis. I also graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science from National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), India. Before coming to the US, I also worked for a year at Adobe, India (where my admiration for C++ began).

I love biking in and around Madison. I have a great mountain bike but I want to start road biking! I also like baking and cooking. I make it a point to try out a new recipe whenever I find time.
